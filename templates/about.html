What is Handwriting Recognition?

DeepHTR is a machine learning project which outputs the text from a scanned handwritten document. DeepHTR works by combining a line segmentation algorithm to separate documents into individual lines, and then passing these lines into a transformer based neural classifier.

Using state of the art neural networks, users can simply upload a picture of a scanned handwritten document, and then receive the text from that document.
